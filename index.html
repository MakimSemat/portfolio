About Me

I'm a passionate Computer Engineering student from Universiti Malaysia Perlis (UniMAP) with a strong interest in web development, IoT, and embedded systems. With a CGPA of 3.37, I've developed a solid foundation in both software and hardware engineering.

My expertise lies in the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js), complemented by modern tools like Vite, Tailwind CSS, and Socket.io. On the hardware side, I'm proficient with Arduino, ESP32, FPGA, and circuit design.

I'm constantly looking for opportunities to expand my knowledge and apply my skills to solve real-world problems. Whether it's developing a web application or designing an embedded system, I approach each project with enthusiasm and attention to detail.
